# Mandioca Hostel - Staff Procedures

> This document outlines daily procedures for staff and volunteers.

---

## Daily Schedule

### Morning (8:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

1. **8:00 AM** - Check cleaning dashboard for assigned tasks
2. **8:30 AM** - Start room cleaning (checkout rooms first)
3. **10:00 AM** - Clean common areas and kitchen
4. **11:00 AM** - Pool and terrace maintenance
5. **12:00 PM** - Final check and mark tasks complete

### Afternoon (12:00 PM - 6:00 PM)

1. **12:00 PM** - Lunch break
2. **1:00 PM** - Guest check-ins begin
3. **2:00 PM** - Restock supplies (toilet paper, soap, towels)
4. **4:00 PM** - Light cleaning of common areas
5. **5:00 PM** - Kitchen check before dinner time

### Evening (6:00 PM - 11:00 PM)

1. **6:00 PM** - Monitor common areas
2. **9:00 PM** - Final check-ins
3. **10:00 PM** - Kitchen closing clean
4. **11:00 PM** - Quiet hours begin - remind guests

## Room Cleaning Procedure

### After Checkout (Deep Clean)

1. ✅ Strip all linens (sheets, pillowcases, towels)
2. ✅ Check under mattress and in corners for forgotten items
3. ✅ Dust all surfaces (shelves, nightstands, windowsills)
4. ✅ Clean mirrors and glass
5. ✅ Make bed with fresh linens
6. ✅ Restock towels
7. ✅ Empty trash bins and replace bags
8. ✅ Sweep floor
9. ✅ Mop floor
10. ✅ Air out room (open windows for 15 min)
11. ✅ Check lights and AC work
12. ✅ Final visual inspection

### Daily Refresh (Occupied Rooms)

1. ✅ Ask guest permission before entering
2. ✅ Make beds (if guest allows)
3. ✅ Empty trash
4. ✅ Replace used towels
5. ✅ Quick floor sweep

---

## Bathroom Cleaning Procedure

1. ✅ Spray disinfectant on all surfaces
2. ✅ Clean toilet (inside and outside)
3. ✅ Clean sink and counter
4. ✅ Clean shower walls and floor
5. ✅ Clean mirrors
6. ✅ Restock toilet paper (always 2 rolls)
7. ✅ Restock soap/shampoo
8. ✅ Mop floor with disinfectant
9. ✅ Check drain is clear
10. ✅ Replace any mold-affected items

---

## Kitchen Cleaning Procedure

### Throughout the Day

- Wash dishes left in sink
- Wipe counters after use
- Clean spills immediately
- Check fridge for expired items (Fridays)

### End of Day (10:00 PM)

1. ✅ Wash all dishes
2. ✅ Wipe down counters and stove
3. ✅ Clean microwave inside
4. ✅ Sweep floor
5. ✅ Mop floor
6. ✅ Take out trash
7. ✅ Restock paper towels

---

## Pool Area Procedure

### Morning (Before Opening)

1. ✅ Check chemical levels (pH, chlorine)
2. ✅ Skim surface for debris
3. ✅ Check pump is working
4. ✅ Arrange pool furniture
5. ✅ Put out fresh towels

### Evening (After Closing)

1. ✅ Skim surface
2. ✅ Stack furniture
3. ✅ Empty trash bins
4. ✅ Lock pool gate (if applicable)

---

## Emergency Procedures

### Medical Emergency

1. Call emergency services: **911**
2. Stay with the guest
3. Notify manager immediately
4. Document the incident

### Fire

1. Sound alarm
2. Help guests evacuate via nearest exit
3. Call **911**
4. Meet at assembly point (front parking)
5. Account for all guests

### Security Issue

1. Do not confront aggressive persons
2. Call police if necessary
3. Document incident
4. Notify manager

## Guest Interaction Guidelines

### Do's

- ✅ Greet guests with a smile
- ✅ Be helpful and patient
- ✅ Knock before entering rooms
- ✅ Respect guest privacy
- ✅ Offer recommendations for local attractions

### Don'ts

- ❌ Enter rooms without permission (except emergencies)
- ❌ Share guest information with others
- ❌ Accept tips (politely decline)
- ❌ Use guest facilities
